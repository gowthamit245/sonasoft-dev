IoT Analytics

IoT Advisory

Internet of things is not just connecting the devices or things anymore. Anblicks can help you build IoT solutions based on your industry, business need, identify critical components and provide advisory services on usability, by developing overall design and architecture of the solution and provide roadmap for implementation. We can also develop a proof of concept or proof of value based on your business need.

Operational Intelligence Analysis Services

Raw data collected from the sensors attached to the machines are fed to big data eco system for further analysis and develop deep learning, machine learning and even build dashboards and real time visualization of data based metrics.
